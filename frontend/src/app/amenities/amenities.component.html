Food Service/ Food Runner

There are many different types of food and beverage service types or procedures, but the major category of the food service is 1) Plate Service, 2) Cart Service, 3) Plater Service, 4) Buffet Service and 5) Family style service.

Refreshment

The refreshment center is full of various snacks and drinks. For example we have bottled water, coke, orange juice, Hersheys chocolate, pretzels, Snickers, Twix etc. It varies from time to time but it is all complimentary when booking the premium suites.

Housekeeping

The housekeeping department works closely with other hotel departments too. The front desk communicates with housekeeping constantly, working to coordinate check-ins and check-outs, and following through with guest requests. Housekeeping staff partner with the engineering department to resolve maintenance issues and fix broken items.

Room Security

Management should consider placing a telephone device in each guest room. This telephone can be used by the guest to notify the property of emergencies or suspicious activities and by the property to notify the guest of emergencies like Fire, Bomb threat etc.

Private Pool

There are many different types of private pool available and beverage service types or procedures, but the major category of the pool service is 1) for two, 2) for family, 3) single
